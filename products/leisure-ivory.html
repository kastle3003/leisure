Leisure Ivory

Hand-finished in Milan

Where the Noir is shadow, the Ivory is light. Twelve coats of hand-applied ivory lacquer, each flatted between applications. Brushed brass grilles. Solid oak base. The Ivory does not simply perform — it presides. Three hundred pairs, each finished over four days by a single craftsperson.

The Philosophy

The Ivory shares its acoustic architecture with the Noir. The same diamond tweeter. The same crossover topology. The same internal bracing geometry. What differs is the character — one is power, the other is presence.

The natural paper and linen cone was chosen for the Ivory specifically — its organic warmth complements the finish without compromising the truth. The brushed brass grille is removable, revealing the raw drivers beneath: tools of extraordinary precision dressed in something beautiful.
