Leisure Arc

Cinematic. Minimal. Flawless.

The Arc reimagines what a soundbar can be. A single continuous curve of aircraft-grade aluminium houses eleven drivers tuned in a custom Leisure configuration. Dolby Atmos. DTS:X. Zero compromise. It sits below your screen like a piece of sculpture and sounds like a full theatre.

The Philosophy

Most soundbars are a compromise. The Arc is not. We refused to accept that cinema sound requires a rack of equipment and a room full of speakers. With eleven precisely angled drivers and Leisure's proprietary room-correction algorithm, the Arc fills any space.

The enclosure is machined from a single billet of aluminium. There are no seams. No rattles. No resonance. Just the sound the director intended.
