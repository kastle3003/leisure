The Philosophy

We spent three years tuning the Leisure One. Not just measuring it — tuning it. There is a difference. Measurements tell you what a speaker does. Only ears tell you what it is.

The beryllium tweeter was chosen after evaluating forty-one alternatives. The walnut cabinet was braced in six different configurations before we found the one that vanished. Every decision was made in service of one goal: the truth.
