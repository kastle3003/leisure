Leisure Noir

300 Pairs. Worldwide.

The Leisure Noir is not a product. It is a statement. Blackened aerospace steel meets hand-laid carbon fibre in a cabinet that absorbs light as completely as it refuses to colour sound. Three hundred pairs. Each numbered. Each a collector's object as much as a reference instrument.

The Philosophy

The Noir began as an internal question: what if cost were no object? What if we could source any material, specify any driver, take any amount of time? The Noir is the answer to that question.

The diamond-coated beryllium tweeter extends response beyond the range of human hearing — not because we needed it to, but because we refused to place a ceiling on what this speaker could do. Each pair is delivered with a certificate of origin, signed by the engineer who built it.
