The Philosophy

We were asked many times to make a portable speaker. We refused, until we could make one we were proud of. The Nomad took two years. The difficulty was not the engineering — it was the honesty.

Most portable speakers pretend. They use processing to simulate bass they cannot physically reproduce. The Nomad does not pretend. It works within its physical limits with absolute integrity, and within those limits, it is extraordinary. Full-grain leather, machined aluminium, a driver array tuned in our Milan atelier. It is the smallest thing we make. It is no less serious.
