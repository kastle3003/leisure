The Philosophy

The Studio was designed alongside professional recording engineers. It had to work in a control room — the most critical listening environment on earth. Only once it passed there did we allow it into the home.

The woven carbon fibre cone gives exceptional stiffness without the brittle resonances of conventional materials. The result is imaging so precise, you can place instruments in a room with your eyes closed.
