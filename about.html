Our Story

The Beginning

Milan, 2019.

Leisure was founded by two acoustic engineers who shared a single frustration: that the pursuit of affordable audio had quietly murdered sonic excellence. They believed a speaker should disappear — not in the room, but in the performance.

Three years of prototyping. Forty-seven driver configurations. One obsession. The result was the Leisure One — and nothing has been the same since.

Our Philosophy

01

Craft

Every cabinet is hand-finished. Every driver hand-measured. Tolerances that machines cannot achieve, only patience can.

02

Purity

No colouration. No signature. Just the recording, exactly as the artist intended it to be heard. Pure signal. Pure truth.

03

Obsession

We don't ship a product until it moves us. If it doesn't make us feel something, it doesn't leave the atelier.
